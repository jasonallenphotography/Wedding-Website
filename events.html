Friday, October 14th, 2016

2:00PM - 3:00PM

Ceremony

George Eastman Museum

We will be getting married outside at the West Gardens (weather permitting!) of the George Eastman Museum in Rochester, New York - home of Kodak founder and photography pioneer George Eastman! In the event of inclement weather, the ceremony will be indoors at the museum's Dryden Theater, accessible from the west side of the building.

Parking is available for free on-site, but we encourage guests to arrive early in the event that the lot is crowded. The museum will be open, and you might have to hunt for a good parking space!

After the ceremony, guests are encouraged by the groom to visit the excellent Eastman photography museum while we run off with our photographer to get our album photos taken! Plenty of down time to head back to your hotel to nap or freshen up for the reception!

Click on the map for directions to the museum. We'll have balloons marking the correct parking lot's entrance!

5:00PM - 11:00PM

Reception

The Inn on Broadway

Following the ceremony (and album photos) there will be an open bar reception at The Inn on Broadway in downtown Rochester. Dinner, dancing, and dessert follow!

Parking is available for free on-site, but we encourage guests to arrive early in the event that the lot is crowded.

Click on the map for directions to the banquet hall. We'll have balloons marking the correct parking lot's entrance!
