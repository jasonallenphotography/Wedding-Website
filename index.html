When and Where is the ceremony?

We will be getting married in the West Gardens (weather permitting!) at the George Eastman Museum in Rochester, New York. In the event of inclement weather, the ceremony will be indoors at the museum's Dryden Theater, accessible from the west side of the building.

Parking is available for free on-site, but we encourage guests to arrive early in the event that the lot is crowded. The museum will be open, and you might have to hunt for a good parking space!
