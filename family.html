Pre-ceremony

George Eastman Museum

Immediate family members (siblings, parents, grandparents) and bridal party/groomsmen only for this part!

We will be getting married at the George Eastman Museum in Rochester, New York - home of Kodak founder and photography pioneer George Eastman!

We will be meeting with our Rabbi and signing our Ketubah (wedding contract) before the ceremony. Please use the West entrance of the Eastman Museum and arrive early!

Ceremony and Photos

George Eastman Museum

The formal ceremony will be in the West Gardens (weather permitting!) of the Eastman Museum grounds. In the event of inclement weather, the ceremony will be indoors at the museum's Dryden Theater, accessible from the west side of the building.

Parking is available for free on-site, but we encourage guests to arrive early in the event that the lot is crowded. The museum will be open, and you might have to hunt for a good parking space!

After the ceremony, we'll all head off with the photographer to get album and group photos taken! Don't worry though, there will be plenty of down time to head back to your hotel to nap or freshen up for the reception!

Click on the map for directions to the museum. We'll have balloons marking the correct parking lot's entrance!
